Sector 104
Dwarka Expressway

Central Park presents an exclusive ultra-luxury residential address in Sector 104, Gurgaon, perfectly located along the rapidly developing Dwarka Expressway. Spanning 10 acres, this landmark development features striking high-rise towers rising up to 29 floors , offering panoramic city views and thoughtfully designed layouts.

With a mix of premium studio residences and 1 BHK apartments, the project redefines contemporary luxury living in one of NCR's most sought-after destinations. Each residence is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, featuring premium finishes and modern amenities.

Life at Central Park Sector 104 is about unmatched comfort and indulgence, with access to a wide range of world-class amenities. From a grand clubhouse, infinity-edge pool, spa and wellness centre, and fine-dining options to sky lounges, landscaped gardens, jogging tracks, indoor recreation zones, a business lounge, and 24/7 concierge services.

The secure gated community ensures complete privacy, safety, and convenience for its residents. Enjoy seamless connectivity via the Dwarka Expressway, with easy access to IGI Airport, Cyber City, NH-48, and major commercial hubs.

Surrounded by reputed schools, healthcare facilities, shopping centres, and modern infrastructure, Central Park Sector 104 Gurgaon stands out as the perfect destination for both end-users and investors seeking premium real estate in a fast-growing corridor.
